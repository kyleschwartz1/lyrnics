Vision and Mission Statements

Vision Statement
Our vision is to revitalize the tedious act of studying, while giving students and teachers an innovative platform for learning.

Mission Statement
Lyrnics is a creative website that allows students to turn dull notes into vibrant songs, which will help middle school to high school students study in a creative and easy way. Lyrnics accomplishes this by creating a platform for crowd sharing, which allows students to share their Lyrnics with each other.
