Vision and Mission Statements

Vision Statement
Our vision is to revitalize the tedious act of studying, whilst giving students and teachers an innovative platform for learning.

Mission Statement
Lyrnics has created a creative website that allows students to turn dull notes into vibrant songs, which will help middle school to high school students study in a creative and easy way. Lyrnics accomplishes this by creating a platform for crowd sharing, which allows students to share their Lyrnics with each other.
